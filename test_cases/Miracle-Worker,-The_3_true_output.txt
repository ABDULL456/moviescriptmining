19. ANNIE TAPS HER BROW. 
ANNIE 0 Well, he keeps digging up that battle. 
ANAGNOS That is not a proper thing to say. Annie, be humble. He extends a gift to her.. You'll need their affection, work- ing with this child. ANNIE, not quite comprehending, looks at gift. A gift with our affection. ANNIE opens a small box and sees a garnet ring. She looks up, blinking, then down.
